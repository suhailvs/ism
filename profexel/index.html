What is Profexel

About Profexel

Profexel is a unique family event tailored specifically for professionals. Organized by ISM Kerala with the support of its professional wing, Team Inspire, this event provides a remarkable opportunity to engage with esteemed scholars. Join us on Sunday, July 7th, at the Orlando City Convention Centre in Thenjipalam, near Calicut University. The event will feature a variety of captivating sessions on Islamic topics, complete with Question and Answer segments, In Sha Allah. Additionally, we have arranged separate activities and sessions for women, children, and teenagers.
In today's world, many perceive maintaining an Islamic identity as a challenge, exacerbated by the prevalence of hatred, intolerance, and non-cooperation within the Islamic community. Concerns about political instability in Islamic countries and debates over the role of political Shi’ism as a counterforce to Zionism also persist. Furthermore, there is a noticeable trend of Muslim youth distancing themselves from their faith, while various countries and corporations exhibit opposition to Islam. Despite these challenges, Islam continues to grow globally.
This event presents a valuable opportunity to address the many questions surrounding these issues. It also offers a chance to network with fellow professionals and their families. We warmly welcome you and your family to attend this event and kindly request that you register in advance to ensure your seat and meal arrangements.
