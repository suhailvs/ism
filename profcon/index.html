About Profcon

Join us for Profocon, the Professional Family Meeting State Conference on July 7th, from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM at the Orlando City Convention Centre in Thenjipalam, Calicut University. This conference is dedicated to exploring and reinforcing Islamic ethical principles and the importance of upright behavior in both our professional and family lives. Through a series of thought-provoking discussions, engaging workshops, and collaborative sessions, Profocon aims to create an environment where integrity, mutual respect, and moral fortitude are at the forefront. Attendees will have the opportunity to share experiences, gain valuable insights, and strengthen their commitment to ethical living.

Profocon is not just a meeting but a pivotal event for nurturing a culture of righteousness and support within our community, guiding us towards collective well-being and success. Don't miss this enriching and inspiring conference!
